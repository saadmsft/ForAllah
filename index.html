Divine Promises from the Qur'an

A collection of Allah's assurances to humanity, supported by the Sunnah.

I. Faith and the Believer's Status

1. The Promise of Paradise (Jannah)

وَعَدَ اللَّهُ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ وَالْمُؤْمِنَاتِ جَنَّاتٍ تَجْرِي مِن تَحْتِهَا الْأَنْهَارُ

"Allah has promised the believing men and believing women gardens beneath which rivers flow..."

Qur'an, 9:72

From the Sunnah:

The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Allah said, 'I have prepared for My righteous slaves (in Paradise) what no eye has seen, no ear has heard, and no human heart can imagine.'"

Sahih al-Bukhari

2. The Promise of a Good Life

مَنْ عَمِلَ صَالِحًا مِّن ذَكَرٍ أَوْ أُنثَىٰ وَهُوَ مُؤْمِنٌ فَلَنُحْيِيَنَّهُ حَيَاةً طَيِّبَةً

"Whoever does righteousness, whether male or female, while he is a believer - We will surely cause him to live a good life..."

Qur'an, 16:97

From the Sunnah:

The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Richness is not in having many possessions, but richness is in the richness of the soul."

Sahih al-Bukhari

II. Promises of Cause and Effect

3. For Seeking Forgiveness (Istighfar)

فَقُلْتُ اسْتَغْفِرُوا رَبَّكُمْ إِنَّهُ كَانَ غَفَّارًا يُرْسِلِ السَّمَاءَ عَلَيْكُم مِّدْرَارًا

"'Ask forgiveness of your Lord... He will send [rain from] the sky upon you in showers...'"

Qur'an, 71:10-11

From the Sunnah:

The Prophet (ﷺ) said, 'Whoever constantly seeks forgiveness, Allah will appoint for him a way out of every distress and a relief from every anxiety, and will provide for him from where he did not expect.'

Sunan Abi Dawood

4. For Gratitude (Shukr)

لَئِن شَكَرْتُمْ لَأَزِيدَنَّكُمْ

"'If you are grateful, I will surely increase you [in favor]...'"

Qur'an, 14:7

From the Sunnah:

The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "He has not thanked Allah who has not thanked people."

Sunan Abi Dawood

5. For Piety (Taqwa)

وَمَن يَتَّقِ اللَّهَ يَجْعَل لَّهُ مَخْرَجًا وَيَرْزُقْهُ مِنْ حَيْثُ لَا يَحْتَسِبُ

"And whoever fears Allah - He will make for him a way out and will provide for him from where he does not expect."

Qur'an, 65:2-3

From the Sunnah:

The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "The most honorable of people is the one with the most Taqwa (piety)."

Sahih al-Bukhari

6. For Trust (Tawakkul)

وَمَن يَتَوَكَّلْ عَلَى اللَّهِ فَهُوَ حَسْبُهُ

"...And whoever relies upon Allah - then He is sufficient for him."

Qur'an, 65:3

From the Sunnah:

The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "If you were to rely upon Allah with the reliance He is due, you would be provided for as a bird is provided for: it goes out in the morning hungry and returns in the evening full."

Jami` at-Tirmidhi

7. For Patience (Sabr)

إِنَّمَا يُوَفَّى الصَّابِرُونَ أَجْرَهُم بِغَيْرِ حِسَابٍ

"Only those who are patient shall receive their reward in full, without reckoning."

Qur'an, 39:10

From the Sunnah:

The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "No one is given a gift better and more comprehensive than patience."

Sahih al-Bukhari

8. For Spending (Infaq)

وَمَا أَنفَقْتُم مِّن شَيْءٍ فَهُوَ يُخْلِفُهُ

"But whatever thing you spend [in His cause] - He will replace it..."

Qur'an, 34:39

From the Sunnah:

The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "There is not a day upon which the servants awaken but that two angels descend. One of them says: 'O Allah, give compensation to the one who spends.' The other says: 'O Allah, give ruin to the one who withholds.'"

Sahih al-Bukhari

III. Promises of Divine Support

9. For Protection & Defense

إِنَّ اللَّهَ يُدَافِعُ عَنِ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا

"Verily Allah will defend (from ill) those who believe..."

Qur'an, 22:38

From the Sunnah:

The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Allah the Exalted has said: 'Whoever takes a Wali (friend) of Mine as an enemy, I will declare war on him...'"

Sahih al-Bukhari

10. An Answered Prayer (Du'a)

وَقَالَ رَبُّكُمُ ادْعُونِي أَسْتَجِبْ لَكُمْ

"And your Lord says, 'Call upon Me; I will respond to you.'"

Qur'an, 40:60

From the Sunnah:

The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "The supplication of any one of you will be answered so long as he is not impatient and says, 'I supplicated, but I was not answered.'"

Sahih al-Bukhari

IV. Promises of Protection and Security

11. Protection from the Evil Eye

مَا شَاءَ اللَّهُ لَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ

"What Allah willed has occurred; there is no power except in Allah."

Qur'an, 18:39

From the Sunnah:

The Prophet (ﷺ) said: "The evil eye is real, and if anything were to overtake the divine decree, it would be the evil eye."

Sahih Muslim

12. Protection from Plots

حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ

"Sufficient for us is Allah, and [He is] the best Disposer of affairs."

Qur'an, 3:173-174

From the Sunnah:

Ibn 'Abbas said: "When Ibrahim (Abraham) was thrown into the fire, he said: 'Allah (Alone) is sufficient for us, and He is the Best Disposer of affairs.' So did Muhammad (ﷺ) when they said, 'A great army is gathering against you, therefore, fear them...'"

Sahih al-Bukhari

13. Protection from People's Harm

وَأُفَوِّضُ أَمْرِي إِلَى اللَّهِ

"And I entrust my affair to Allah."

Qur'an, 40:44-45

From the Sunnah:

The Prophet (ﷺ) said: "Be mindful of Allah, and you will find Him in front of you. If you ask, ask of Allah; if you seek help, seek help of Allah."

Jami` at-Tirmidhi

14. Protection from Distress & Grief

لَّا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا أَنتَ سُبْحَانَكَ إِنِّي كُنتُ مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ

"There is no deity except You; exalted are You. Indeed, I have been of the wrongdoers."

Qur'an, 21:87-88

From the Sunnah:

The Prophet (ﷺ) said: "The supplication of Dhun-Nun (Prophet Yunus) which he supplicated while in the belly of the whale... no Muslim man supplicates with it for anything, but Allah will grant it to him."

Jami` at-Tirmidhi
